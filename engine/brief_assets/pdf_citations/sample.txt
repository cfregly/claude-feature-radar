  PDF citations: a verifiable pointer to the exact page of a directly-supplied PDF.
  Five questions over a five-page SaaS Pro Plan agreement, supplied as a base64 PDF in the request.

  question                                    cited page   right page   cost
  -----------------------------------------------------------------------------
  How many seats are included?                       1          yes
  How much is each overage seat per month?           2          yes
  What discount do annual contracts get?             3          yes
  How many days of notice to terminate?              4          yes
  What is the monthly uptime commitment?             5          yes
  -----------------------------------------------------------------------------
  answered 5/5      correct page 5/5      $0.0458 on Claude Haiku 4.5

  The change: send the PDF as a base64 document block and set citations enabled. Each answer comes
  back with a page_location pointer (page number plus the quoted source text, free of output tokens),
  so your app deep-links to the source page with zero resolver code.
